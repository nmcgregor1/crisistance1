About Crisistance

Our mission is to empower small businesses to face crises with confidence and respond with purpose.

Mission & Values

Crisistance combines proven crisis management strategies with personal guidance. We help you prepare, respond, and recover — so your business stays resilient.

How It Works

  1. Assess: We map your risks and critical processes.
  2. Plan: We develop practical playbooks and communication templates.
  3. Enable: We train teams and run tabletop exercises.
  4. Support: We stand by during incidents and coordinate recovery.

Credibility

Experience across technology, public sector, and small‑business operations. References available on request.
